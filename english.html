Basketball Profile

Kiril Cvetkovski is a Macedonian basketball player from Skopje born on 03.04.2012, at a height of a 159cm/5.2Ft and 41kg/90pounds.Kiril plays for BC Feniks as a point guard he wears the number 7.Kiril has played in many competitions.Kiril and his club have many victories in the Macedonian category U12 wining one final in the season of 2022/2023. He trains with his coach all the time.The most recent competition was IBC competition in Belgrade where Feniks won 4th place.

Karate profile

Kiril fought karate 4 years in the karate club KK Metalurg he was a international champion of the year 2021 in the category 40-kilos 2012. In the year 2021 Kiril and his club went to a lot of competitions like the Balkan championship for kids and a lot more. In 2020 he was on the international championship of Macedonia where he won 2nd place.In 2020 and 2021 on the Balkan championships Kiril won 4th and 5th place.Kiril quitted karate in the summer of the year 2022.Kiril's last competition in karate was the Balkan championship for kids 2022.
